Agridera's Fiery Harvest Hot Pepper Varieties

×××××

At Agridera, our mission is to revolutionize the hot pepper industry by producing top-tier seeds tailored to meet the demands of growers and consumers worldwide. Through relentless research and innovation, we strive to offer solutions that elevate agricultural practices and delight taste buds across the globe.

Our primary focus lies in developing hot pepper cultivars with robust resistance to prevalent diseases like Pepper Mild Mottle Virus (PMMoV) and Phytophthora blight. We're dedicated to creating varieties that thrive in diverse climates and conditions, ensuring a bountiful harvest for farmers everywhere.

In addition to disease resistance, we prioritize horticultural traits such as early maturation, varied pepper sizes, firm texture, vibrant color, intense flavor, crack resistance, extended shelf life, and adaptability to both high and low temperatures. Our portfolio encompasses a range of pepper shapes, from traditional round to slender and elongated, each boasting a unique combination of quality attributes and fiery heat.

At Agridera, our dedication to excellence extends beyond tomatoes to encompass the thrilling world of hot peppers. We've strategically structured our breeding programs around three key categories: Greenhouse Indeterminate Peppers, Open-field Indeterminate Peppers, and Bush Type Open-field Peppers.

Greenhouse Hot Pepper
Cultivation Strategy

Our greenhouse-focused breeding program targets the discerning markets of Spain and Turkey. We prioritize the production of both single and cluster pick varieties, boasting round fruit shapes and fortified against a spectrum of threats including Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl Virus (TYLCV), Tomato Spotted Wilt Virus (TSWV), nematodes, and Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. radicis-lycopersici (FORL). Some of our hybrids are engineered with additional safeguards, such as resistance to Cladosporium and Leveillula Taurica (Lv).

Open-field Indeterminate
Hot Peppers

Tailored for the vibrant markets of Brazil, Morocco, and Turkey. Our open-field breeding endeavors are dedicated to cultivating large, round-shaped fruits weighing over 200 grams. These varieties are engineered to withstand the onslaught of TYLCV, TSWV, nematodes, and FORL. In our continuous pursuit of excellence, future iterations will also integrate resistance against Fusarium oxysporum f.sp. lycopersici (F3) as a foundational trait in our cultivars.

Open-field Bush
Type Hot Peppers

Our bush type hot pepper breeding program caters to the tastes of the Mediterranean markets. We meticulously craft varieties in both round and elongated shapes, available in standard sizes ranging from 140 to 180 grams, as well as beefier sizes exceeding 180 grams, up to 300 grams and beyond. These hybrids are fortified with core resistances against TYLCV, TSWV, and nematodes. For added protection, select hybrids are fortified with additional defenses, including resilience against Cladosporium and Leveillula Taurica (Lv).

Agridera collaborates with a network of international and local distributors spanning the globe.
